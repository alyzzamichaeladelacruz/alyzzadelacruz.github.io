Kalan + Banga

The Calamba Banga is a famous landmark in Calamba City, Laguna, symbolizing the city’s name and history. The word Calamba comes from the Tagalog words "kalan" (stove) and "banga" (clay pot), inspired by a legend where locals pointed to a clay pot when Spanish friars asked about the town’s name. The Calamba Banga represents the city's identity and its rich cultural heritage.

Jose Rizal Monument

The José Rizal Monument in Calamba City, Laguna is a towering tribute to the Philippines’ national hero, Dr. José Rizal, located in his hometown. The José Rizal Monument stands as a powerful symbol of patriotism and a reminder of Rizal’s contributions to Philippine independence.

Jose Rizal Colesium

The José Rizal Coliseum is a multipurpose sports and event venue in Calamba City, Laguna, named in honor of the national hero, Dr. José Rizal. The José Rizal Coliseum serves as a hub for sports, culture, and entertainment in Calamba City.

Rizal Shrine

The José Rizal Shrine in Calamba City, Laguna, is a historical museum and national landmark commemorating the life and legacy of Dr. José Rizal, the Philippines’ national hero. The José Rizal Shrine is a treasured reminder of Rizal’s contributions to Philippine independence and his enduring impact on Filipino identity.

Buhayani Festival

The Buhayani Festival is an annual celebration in Calamba City, Laguna that honors the legacy of Dr. José Rizal and promotes the values of heroism (buhay ng bayani) The Buhayani Festival is a vibrant tribute to Dr. Rizal’s enduring influence and the heroism he represents.

Calamba City Hall

The Calamba City Hall is a prominent government building in Calamba City, Laguna, known for its modern design and its role as the center of local governance. Calamba City is a vibrant mix of history, culture, and modern development, making it one of the most dynamic cities in the Philippines.

City Profile

Calamba City: The Heart of Laguna Calamba City is a vibrant destination where history and modernity blend seamlessly. Known as the birthplace of national hero Dr. Jose Rizal, the city boasts a rich cultural heritage alongside thriving urban development. Surrounded by lush landscapes and soothing hot springs, it’s perfect for relaxation and adventure. With its welcoming community and dynamic spirit, Calamba offers travelers a unique mix of tradition, nature, and progress.
